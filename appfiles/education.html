Education

Working in education covers a wide range of age groups. You could work in early childhood education – in kindergartens, kōhanga reo or care centres. You could become a competent, knowledgeable and reflective early childhood teacher who is committed to young children and their learning and development.

You’ll have skills to help young children develop a love of learning and make a difference to their lives. You could even go into educational management, to improve teaching standards and academic procedures.

Early childhood teachers work in government-run kindergartens, childcare centres, home-based care agencies, or kōhanga reo.

Career Options

Unitec offers a wide range of courses relating to Education. The following career opportunities are all possible options after studying at Unitec:

  • Early Childhood Teacher
  • Head Teacher in an Early Childhood Setting
  • Home-Based Caregiver


  • Manager in an Early Childhood Education Centre
