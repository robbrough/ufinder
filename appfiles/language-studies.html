Language Studies

Speaking more than one language can open doors. Fluent speakers of other languages can use their knowledge to work as an interpreter, working in a variety of situations converting spoken statements from one language to another, and from one culture to another. They can also learn the skills to teach a language in New Zealand or around the world!

Around 83% of New Zealanders only speak one language and around 98% are English speakers.

Career Options

Unitec offers a wide range of courses relating to Language Studies. The following career opportunities are all possible options after studying at Unitec:

  • English language teacher overseas
  • Liaison interpreter
