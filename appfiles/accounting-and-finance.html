Accounting and Finance

Accounting and finance professionals are key to any organisation. They develop and operate financial systems, manage tax obligations and cash flows, and provide input into strategic planning.

It’s an exciting career with lots of variety, good job security and impressive salaries. Forget the image of the number cruncher and scorekeeper – the new breed of accountant is a forward-looking business leader. And it’s a career that can take you right around the world.

More than 62,500 people work in the finance, accounting and insurance industry in New Zealand.
