Architecture

Architecture is not just a job. It’s not just a craft. And it’s not just about creating beautiful houses. It’s about reshaping the world. With buildings that are smarter, have a small ecological footprint, and feature thought-provoking designs.

You’ll plan, design and advise on the construction of new buildings, as well as making improvements and additions to existing buildings. Working on all kinds of buildings – from offices to new housing, historic buildings to schools.

Architectural graduates have to complete 140 weeks of practical work experience to become a registered architect.
