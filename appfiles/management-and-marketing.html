Management and Marketing

Management professionals can be found across an organisation, from Human Resources andOperations to Sales and Marketing. You can work in almost any industry and be part of some of the biggest companies in the world.

You will help shape an organisation, ensure strategic and business plans are in place, financial performance is achieved, and provide leadership and motivation to others.

Experienced marketing managers can earn between $80,000 and $140,000.
