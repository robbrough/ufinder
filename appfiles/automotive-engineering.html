Automotive Engineering

Automotive technicians check, service and repair motor vehicles and their parts and systems. They work on cars, vans, trucks and other light vehicles.

Recovery in the automotive industry creating demand

Demand for automotive technicians has been increasing because of an increase in the number of new car sales and registrations. An overall increase in the number of cars registered in New Zealand. A Department of Labour survey of online vacancies showed an increase in vacancies for motor mechanics (including automotive technicians) of 57% between December 2009 and December 2010.

There are over four million registered vehicles in New Zealand and the biggest area of employment is servicing and repairing them!
