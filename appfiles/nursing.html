Nursing

Nurses work all over the world in many different areas of medicine. From babies through to elderly people, nurses provide healthcare in hospitals, doctors' surgeries, schools, specialist clinics and rehabilitation centres. And due to the aging population, more and more people are using health services in New Zealand.

New Zealand has over 45,000 registered nurses but more are needed to fill all the job vacancies.

Career Options

High tech nursing
