Performing and Screen Arts

The film and theatre industry employs thousands of people in all sorts of different roles. Writers, actors, dancers, camera operators, costume designers, musicians, composers and technicians – they are all critical to producing shows for stage and screen. You could work in television, film or advertising, or be involved in stage productions of plays, dance performances or music recitals.

Dance became a compulsory part of the school curriculum in 2003 and the number of dance teachers has grown since then.
