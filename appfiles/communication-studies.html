Communication and Event Management

Good communication is important to all businesses. People who work in communications are responsible for getting the company's message out to customers, to staff and to related organisations.

Communications jobs can involve producing newsletters or reports, corresponding with the media (TV, newspapers and radio), or working on events. It's a good career choice if you enjoy researching and analysing information and you have good writing and presenting skills.

Communications Studies qualifications can lead to careers in event management, PR, journalism and marketing.
