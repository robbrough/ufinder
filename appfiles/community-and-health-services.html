Community and Health Services

Want to make a difference to your community? Working in health services means working with individuals, families or groups to help and support the community.

You could be working in mental health, with youth groups, as a social worker or supporting disabled people. Or you could play a part to increase a not-for-profit organisation's effectiveness. Developing strategies for raising funds, managing staff and volunteers, and building effective governance.

Health service managers are responsible for running hospitals, clinics or community health services.

Career Options

Unitec offers a wide range of courses relating to Community and Health Services. The following career opportunities are all possible options after studying at Unitec:

  • Community Educator
  • Community Worker
  • Group Facilitator
  • Volunteer or Youth Worker
  • Not-For-Profit Manager or Leader
