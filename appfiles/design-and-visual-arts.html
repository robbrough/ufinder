Design and Visual Arts

Visual arts and design jobs may involve designing and producing artworks such as prints, carvings, paintings, textiles, pottery or weaving, sign writing, preparing art, images or graphic designs for books or magazines, and creating images or animation for film or television.

It can involve developing products from concept to market and can include electronic products and medical equipment, to furniture, homewares and garden tools and producing prototypes from your own computer models using the latest design software.

Design and visual arts jobs are increasing because the New Zealand film industry is getting bigger and specific web design skills are needed.
