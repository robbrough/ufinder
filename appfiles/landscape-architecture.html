Landscape Architecture

Landscape architects have the vision and skills to transform the spaces that shape our world. They bring together art and science, history and modern technology to a range of projects, from courtyard gardens to luxury resorts and city spaces. Working on a smaller scale, Home Garden designers draw garden designs and make plant selections to build backyard oases.

Landscape architects are accredited by the New Zealand Institute of Landscape Architecture (NZILA).
