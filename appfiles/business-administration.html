Business Administration

A busy office without an administrator? Unthinkable. Banking, invoicing, reception duties, organising meetings, filing and word processing - business administrators are the heart of most offices. They work with staff from across the company, and are usually the first to greet visitors and clients. And they keep the office running smoothly and efficiently.
