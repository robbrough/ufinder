Osteopathy

Osteopathy is a form of healthcare that focuses on problems with the body's neuromusculoskeletal system. Osteopaths treat people of all ages, backgrounds and with all sorts of problems like back, neck, joint pain, headaches, whiplash and tennis elbow.
