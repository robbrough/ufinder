Need Help?

YouFinder is an application to help you make decisions about your career path. It's based on a popular model for identifying personality types called RIASEC.

YouFinder lets you explore different characters to help you find career options that suit your interests and skills. Don't worry if you can't find an exact match - you're probably going to be a mix of a few different personality types so make sure you check them all out.
